🌱 Learning in Public | AI/ML Journey

I am currently learning Data Science and AI with the goal of 
becoming an AI Engineer. As part of my learning journey, 
I completed a 3-day advanced EDA sprint on:

**E-Commerce Customer Analytics: RFM Segmentation, Cohort 
Retention & Market Basket Insights**

The goal was to move beyond basic charts and analyze customer 
behavior from a business decision-making perspective.

What I covered:

- Cleaned transactional e-commerce data using Pandas
- Built revenue, month, cohort, and customer-level features
- Analyzed monthly sales trends, top products, and order behavior
- Created RFM scores for customer segmentation
- Segmented customers into Champions, Loyal Customers, New 
Customers, About to Sleep, At Risk, and Lost Customers
- Built a cohort retention heatmap to understand repeat 
purchase behavior
- Identified frequently bought-together products for 
cross-selling opportunities
- Used Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly for polished static 
and interactive visuals

Key learning:

EDA becomes much more valuable when charts are connected to 
business actions. Instead of only asking "what happened?", 
I focused on "what should the business do next?"

Tools used:

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, 
Jupyter Notebook

Still learning, still growing — one project at a time! 🚀
